About Me

Hi! My name is Brad and I am a self-taught front end developer based near London. I particularly relish working with Javascript and React.js. I consider myself an 'eternal student' and enjoy expanding my knowledge and challenging myself with new goals.

I decided to learn about coding while I was working on my Maths degree. I specifically remember turning to reddit for new hobby inspiration and seeing coding suggested. I followed Freecodecamp's front end curriculum to start with and after much dabbling, I decided I wanted to make it my career. I finished my degree and knuckled down, working hard to hone my skills and expand my knowledge base. Coding appeals to my enjoyment of a mental challenge and provides endless opportunities to learn.

I want to work as a front end developer, but within that scope (Javascript pun intended) I'm open to pretty much anything! My favourite part is working with Javascript which offers great power to solve many problems. I've also started branching out into the back end learning Node.js, Express.js and MongoDB. It would be very cool to end up as full stack developer and being able jump around the stack to keep things fresh and varied.
